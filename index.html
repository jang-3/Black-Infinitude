The Problem

People throw away perfectly good furniture. Furniture is wasted and reduced to trash, when it's value could be much more to others.

But what if there was a way to optimise the value of unwanted furniture? That’s where Interbarter comes in – an innovative platform that connects people willing to part with their unwanted items with those looking for something similar.

About Us

Based in Singapore, we strive for furniture to go unwasted and making the best use for it. We help others around Singapore to use other's furniture, that they may enjoy

Why Interbarter?

Share

Give to Earn

When you use Interbarter, by giving away unwanted furniture, you receive Clover coins that allow you to purchase other second-hand furniture provided by other users when your goods are purchased. In other words, you gain something by losing nothing.
